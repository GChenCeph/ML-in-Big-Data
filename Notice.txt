Temperature Data:

climdiv-tmaxst-vx.y.z-YYYYMMDD: Statewide/regional/national monthly maximum temperature.
climdiv-tminst-vx.y.z-YYYYMMDD: Statewide/regional/national monthly minimum temperature.
climdiv-tmpcst-vx.y.z-YYYYMMDD: Statewide/regional/national average temperature.
Precipitation Data:

climdiv-pcpnst-vx.y.z-YYYYMMDD: Statewide/regional/national monthly precipitation data.
Drought Indices:

climdiv-pdsist-vx.y.z-YYYYMMDD: Palmer Drought Severity Index (PDSI).
climdiv-phdist-vx.y.z-YYYYMMDD: Palmer Hydrological Drought Index (PHDI).
climdiv-zndxst-vx.y.z-YYYYMMDD: Palmer "Z" Index (ZNDX).
climdiv-sp01st-vx.y.z-YYYYMMDD to climdiv-sp24st-vx.y.z-YYYYMMDD: Standardized Precipitation Index for various time scales (1 to 24 months).
